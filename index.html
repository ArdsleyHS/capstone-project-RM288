Polar Bear Charity

This is a website dedicated to the CPB (charity for polar bears). Here at CPB, we strive to raise money to help save the polar bears. Donations will be used to perserve polar bear habitats, such as through the use of state-of-the-art freeze enhancement devices. Another apporach we talk is the funding of an atmospheric landscaping projection that will injet harmless (FDA Approval Awaiting), greenhouse-gas eliminating elements into the air above polar bear habitats, slowing the damage caused by global warming without harming the bears.

Ways in which donating helps

  • Helps minimize habitat damage
  • Helps perserve living polar bears
  • Allows the funding of reactive and preventative methods
  • Allows for funding of rallies
  • Increases ability of charity to draw new followers
  • Helps fund creation of brand new technologies to prevent further damage
  • Gradualy funding creation of interstellar satellite with internally maintained artifical environemnt that can last 100 thousand years and be filled with polar bears before launching into deep space to find a new home planet.
